# Naples Pest Experts — Rank & Rent Monetization Strategy

## Overview
The "Naples Pest Experts" website is built as a high-authority Lead Generation asset. The goal is to rank for lucrative keywords like "Termite Control Naples" and "Bed Bug Treatment Naples" and then lease the leads or the entire site to a local pest control operator in Southwest Florida.

## Monetization Models

### 1. Pay-Per-Lead (Standard Start)
*   **Qualified Call (2min+):** $45 - $65 depending on service (Termite leads are premium).
*   **Contact Form Submission:** $25 - $35.
*   **Tracking:** Use CallRail or Twilio to track call duration and record conversations for quality verification.

### 2. Monthly Flat-Fee Rent (Scale Strategy)
*   Once the site hits Page 1 for broad terms (Pest Control Naples), the site is leased at a flat fee of **$1,500 - $3,000 per month**.
*   **Includes:** Dynamic phone number forwarding, form notifications, and brand exclusivity for that city.

### 3. Revenue Share (High Trust)
*   **Commision:** 10-15% of gross revenue from leads closed via the site.
*   **Pros:** Much higher ceiling (Termite jobs can be $3,000+).
*   **Cons:** Requires deep trust and full CRM access for auditing.

## Lead Generation Tactics
*   **Click-to-Call Dominance:** Phone numbers are sticky in the header and fixed at the bottom on mobile.
*   **AEO (SGE) Optimization:** "Answer Blocks" are embedded in every page to capture AI-driven searches (Gemini/Perplexity).
*   **Locality Signals:** Neighborhood lists (Park Shore, Pelican Bay, etc.) anchor the site as a "hyper-local" authority.
*   **Conversion Psychology:** Use of "Emergency" and "Same Day" terminology to drive urgency.

## Technical Setup for Handover
*   **Email Forwarding:** Form submissons are routed to the renter's CRM.
*   **Phone Integration:** Use the +1 239-555-0174 tracking number to measure performance invisibly.
*   **SEO Stewardship:** Monthly content updates (Blog) maintain rankings.
